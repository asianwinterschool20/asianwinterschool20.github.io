About

The Asian Winter School (AWS) on Strings, Particles, and Cosmology is a pan-Asian initiative by high-energy theorists from China, India, Japan, and Korea. It provides young researchers in Asia an opportunity to learn about recent developments in high-energy theory from leading experts. Now in its 20th edition, the school is aimed at advanced graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and active researchers. It offers a platform for participants to engage in discussions with world experts and to foster collaborations with peers from different countries, continuing a tradition of building a vibrant regional community in theoretical physics. The organisers are indebtedly grateful to the Murty Trust for generously funding the current Indian chapter of the Asian Winter School.
